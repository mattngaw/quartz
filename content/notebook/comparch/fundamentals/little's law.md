$$
\text{To find } L \text{, the number of items in the system at steady-state.}\\
$$
$$

L = \lambda w\\

$$
$$
\begin{matrix}
\lambda \text{, the arrival rate of new items.}\\
w \text{, the time spent by each item in the system.}
\end{matrix}
$$
